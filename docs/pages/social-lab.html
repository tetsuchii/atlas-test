OTP Bank asked us to create the foundation for a long-term Social Lab: a new organisational model designed to help the bank respond to complex social challenges in a more coordinated, proactive, and impact-driven way. Our role was to build the full framework—from the strategic intent to the operational structure—ensuring the Lab becomes a credible engine for social innovation inside the bank.

Click to see images

Over several months, we worked with a dedicated cross-departmental team, running a series of workshops, interviews, and expert consultations. These sessions explored OTP’s existing social activities, internal expectations, ESG commitments, and ecosystem relationships. We mapped the bank’s touchpoints across NGOs, municipalities, academia, and rural communities to understand where the Lab could create real, systemic value.

Based on this research, we defined the Lab’s governance model, methodological backbone, manifesto, impact areas, and operating principles. We produced the official Playbook, the internal onboarding materials, and a complete concept for piloting and scaling the Lab—from the first MVP projects to long-term integration into the bank’s strategy and Responsible Banking commitments.

The resulting framework enables OTP to shift from fragmented, donation-led activities to a structured, measurable, and participatory model that supports real solutions for financial inclusion, local communities, and climate-related vulnerabilities.

OUR INVOLVEMENT

  • Research, interviews, and ecosystem mapping
  • Workshop design and facilitation across departments
  • Governance and operating model development
  • Methodology, principles, and manifesto creation
  • Full Playbook and onboarding materials

OUTCOME

We delivered a validated, organisation-ready Social Lab framework with clear governance, guidance, and processes. This gave OTP a coherent model for initiating, testing, and scaling socially impactful projects that integrate existing expertise and ecosystem strengths.

IMPACT

The Social Lab creates a pathway for OTP to move toward proactive, measurable social value creation—supporting financial inclusion, community resilience, and long-term ESG goals while strengthening partnerships across society.
