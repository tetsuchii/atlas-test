Nautech approached us to support the development of their new vessel management product — a solution designed for real-time monitoring and smarter maritime operations.

Click to see images

We collaborated with their team to shape the business model, design the physical product, and create the UX for both the chartplotter and the connected monitoring system. Our focus was on translating complex operational data into a clear, intuitive, and reliable user experience for demanding marine environments.

In parallel, we developed the conceptual brand identity, inspired by the fluidity and rhythm of the sea. Using generative design, we built a visual language that reflects Nautech’s connection to nature while expressing technological precision and innovation.

The result is a cohesive and user-centered solution that positions Nautech as a forward-thinking player in the maritime technology space.

OUR INVOLVEMENT

  • Business model definition and service design support
  • UX design for the chartplotter and vessel monitoring system
  • Physical product design concept
  • Brand identity concept and generative design direction
  • Workshops and co-creation sessions with Nautech’s in-house experts

OUTCOME

The collaboration resulted in a unified product and brand concept that connects usability, aesthetics, and strategic vision. The UX design simplified complex data interactions, improving clarity and control for vessel operators, while the brand direction established a distinctive visual identity rooted in the dynamics of the sea.

IMPACT

The project helped bring the Nautech brand to life, defining its identity, strengthening user trust, and positioning the company as a modern, innovation-driven player in maritime technology.
